Do's Don'ts
Wash your hands regularly. Avoid Close Contact With Anyone.
Cover Your Mouth & Nose. Do Not Spit.
Consult A Doctor If Sick. Avoid Using Public Transport.
Stay Indoors. Do Not Use Over The Counter Medicines.
Take adequate sleep and rest. Don’t Panic, Take It Easy.
Drink plenty of water/liquids and eat nutritious food. Don’t Touch Your Face.
Stay more than one arm's length distance from persons sick with flu. Disposal of used napkin or tissue paper in open areas.
